Know About Me

Hi! I’m the third of four children, and I share a particularly close bond with my eldest sister—I often confide in her things I wouldn’t share with my other siblings. I graduated with a BS in Industrial Engineering from Bulacan State University, Class of 2020, where my love for mathematics, analytical thinking, and design flourished. My career is driven by the immense fulfillment I get when organizing activities, especially when collaborating with Local Government Units. After graduating, I started my first job in November 2020 as a Training Specialist I at the DSWD Academy Facility. A major mentor in my career has been Ms. Mary Joy dela Cruz Suñas, who guided me during my early days. I aim to further develop my communication and analytical skills to become even more effective in my role.
